How to do the Mafia

Objective

Work together to kill off all the Mafia or get killed by them. The game ends when either the Mafia is killed or the Mafia kills everybody else.

Player Types

  1. Moderator: The narrator/referee who runs the game. He/she must make sure the game runs smooth, take votes, and narrate who dies (generally the person who knows the rules best)
  2. Mafia: in-game faction who can kill a single person of choice at night. During the first night, they only take note of the other members of the Mafia. The number of Mafia members is equal to 1/3 of the amount of players
  3. Villagers: Innocent bystanders who can only vote on people to lynch
  4. Doctor: Player (preferably with a PhD) who can choose one person to save each night (meaning that person will not die if chosen by Mafia/Vigilante)
  5. Cop: This Play may inspect one person per night and find out whether they are Mafia or not
  6. Vigilante: Every night this player, like the Mafia, has the ability to kill off one person of choice
Note: these special abilities are used only during night!

Start of the game

Each player is given a role at random except the chosen Moderator. Each person may only look at the role randomly given to him/her. There are two cycles in the game, night and day, starting with night.

  • Night: Each night, all players must keep their heads down and eyes closed unless told otherwise by Moderator. Once the Moderator finishes directing each special role, the day cycle starts again.
  • Moderator: One group at a time, you tell each a special player (ex: Mafia, Cop) to lift their head(s) until they pick a person to perform their action. Repeat the process for each other special player
  • Day: All players have 5 minutes to discuss who to kill. Everybody may nominate to kill another. If so the person nominated may have a chance to defend themselves. If a second player agrees/seconds the nomination, then everybody must vote to execute the person or not. If the 5 minute timer runs out, the cycle ends and it is night.
  • Voting: Each player puts their head down and when the moderator says so, puts their thumb up or down in agreement or disagreement respectively. MAJORITY WINS, Equal votes means the person lives!
